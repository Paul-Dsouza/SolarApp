PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

Empowering 1 crore households with free 300 units/month of electricity via rooftop solar systems. Government investment: ₹75,000 crore.

Benefits Overview

🏠 Residential Subsidy

  • ₹30,000/kW (up to 2 kW)
  • ₹18,000/kW (next 1 kW)
  • ₹78,000 max subsidy

🏢 GHS / RWA Subsidy

  • ₹18,000/kW (up to 500 kW)
  • Includes EV charging & rooftop

🌄 Special State Boost

  • Extra 10% subsidy per kW
  • For special-category states

🔋 Recommended Solar Capacity

Monthly Usage (Units) Suggested Capacity
0 – 150 1 – 2 kW
150 – 300 2 – 3 kW
300+ Above 3 kW
