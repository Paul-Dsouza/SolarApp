Chief Minister Solar Rooftop Program

Empowering homes through clean energy. The CM Solar Rooftop Scheme offers state-backed support to help households install rooftop solar systems, reducing electricity bills and carbon footprint.

About the Scheme

The CM Rooftop Solar Program is an initiative to promote solar adoption across the state. It offers attractive incentives for residential installations, including simplified approvals, financial assistance, and access to verified vendors.

Key Benefits

🌞 State Subsidy

Up to ₹20,000 per kW for residential users(add proper Subsidy)

📝 Easy Application

Streamlined online registration and approvals

🔌 Reduced Bills

Lower monthly electricity expenses for years

🌱 Environment Friendly

Contribute to a greener, cleaner future
